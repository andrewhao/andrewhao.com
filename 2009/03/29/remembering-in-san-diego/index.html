Remembering in San Diego

Mar 29th, 2009

I’m here in San Diego with my Mom and sisters, just hanging out. I know I lose man-points for saying this, but I’ve really missed them.

Remember flying kites in the backyard? Isn’t Dad such a character? Remember that cherry tree in the backyard? And the swing we put on it? We miss your cooking so much, Mom. We’ve been looking forward to it forever. Remember that pinata at Esther’s birthday party like… 12 years ago? Man. What a sunset.
