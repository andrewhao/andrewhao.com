The Sanity List

Sep 27th, 2007

Whenever life gets crazy and I feel wont to complain, I draft a list of things I’m really thankful for. Some things keeping me sane this week:

  1. Smiles, laughter and witty repartee.

  2. Shamelessly printing on 24lb paper (none of that cheap 20lb stuff!)—in color!

  3. Boatloads of free pens at the career fair (and that sweet Firefox t-shirt that made it all worth it).

  4. A fleeting memory of a feeling (or a feeling of a memory) of how Jesus is worth it. I’m getting back to that place.

  5. Hardwood basketball courts.
