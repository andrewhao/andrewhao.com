Photos - Open Mic at Blake's on Telegraph

Jul 7th, 2008

Early in April we stopped by Blake’s on Telegraph to hear our friends Vicky and Kenny perform during one of their Monday evening open mic’s. Lots of wannabe punk rockers, sweet acoustic artists and screaming ensued before we got to the duo but boy, were they amazing. Go pick up Kenny’s EP, okay?
